Privacy Policy for Schultility

Last Updated: 03.12.2025

Our Promise to You

Your privacy is top tier, as the extension (at the moment) is designed to save your data *only* on your own device.

1. What Data gets saved

We only save the data necessary for the extension to work:

  • Your saved settings, like what grades are toggled on for calculation.

  • Any Data that the

    injected.js

    script extracts from schulnetz.

2. Where Your Data Is Stored

All data is saved locally on your computer or device.

  • It is stored in your browser's local storage.
  • Your data never leaves your device. This extension does not use servers, tracking, or analytics.
